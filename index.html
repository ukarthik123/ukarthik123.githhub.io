    What is the connection between Genes and APIs?

    Since April 2003, scientists have been able to sequence and digitize the human genome. DNA has gone digital. The ability to conduct research on these digitized data predominantly has been conducted by major corporations and universities. Recent advancements in the open source consortium have extended these capabilities to the everyday researcher and clinician.

    ​

    APIs are a programming interface allowing for effective application development and data processing through real-time software-based services.

    ​

    At Gene APIs, we catalog, extend, and develop new APIs and software utilities for researchers engaging in projects related to genomic sequencing.
